OUR FEATURES

We have a vision to make the business online in Nepal. There is a trouble for buying and selling goods of individual due to lack of communication and connectivity. Our mission is to provide a suitable environment so that the people connect to each other.

Our Services

OLSNepal is a free classified ad site where everyone can buy,sell, keeps secondhand or brand new vehicles,shops,rooms,electronics and its applainces,furnitures and many more on rent.Users can also find/post job and provide paid services locally or nationally.

Our Objectives

  • To facilitate transaction from customer to customer.
  • To make the business activities of the seller and the customer smooth and effective.
  • To inform the locals people about the nearest service provider (organization or individual).
  • To get the information about the vacant jobs and post posted by different person or organization.
