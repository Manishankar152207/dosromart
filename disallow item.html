Items/Services not allowed on OLSNepal

OLSNepal does not allow to post ads that are considered illegal as per Nepalese law and/or are not permitted as per below Ad posting rules. Any user found violating these rules will be subjected to action/s mentioned in Terms and Condition Page.
  • OLSNepal don't allowed to post ad like Admissions including Direct Admissions in colleges or institutes or universities.
  • OLSNepal don't allowed to post ad like adoption of Children & Babies.
  • Adult/Sexually Oriented Material are not allowed.
  • Child pornographic material are not allowed.
  • Alcohol,Cigarette, and Tobacco Products are strictly Prohibited on this plathform.
  • Any kind of Allopathic medicine(new or used medicine).
  • Any kind of animal body part of animal skin and bone or any product made of such material.
  • Astrology/Any kind of spiritual or supernatural services or remedies like black magic. Any services where facts are not public and can’t be scientifically ascertained in general.
  • Brand new phones/newly launched/recently purchased without a bill, Jio Phones, etc.
  • Chemicals i.e. “Hazardous Chemicals, Pharmaceutical products, Restricted”, and “Radioactive materials & substances” are not allowed.
  • Child labor or Slavery i.e. Any services which fall within the ambit of child welfare laws of the country.
  • Ad related to Crypto-currency (example:- Bitcoin, bitcoin mining rigs & affiliated services/products) are not allowed.
  • Database - Selling of user database or leads or any information collected from the customers which can benefit others knowingly or unknowingly are not allowed.
  • Ad like Dating services like looking for relationship, dating, and/or friendship.
  • Discriminating on the basis of race, caste, religion, or creed mentioning in ads.
  • Ad lke Education certificates(fake) for High school diplomas, university, medals, etc are not allowed.
  • Escort Services or cross-gender massage are not allowed.
  • Firecrackers, Explosives and Explosive Substances.
  • Free services of any kind with no clearly defined benefits.
  • Government IDs and Licenses Services.
  • Blood, Human Parts etc are not allowed.
  • Items that hurt the Laws for the Protection of Historical, Artistic, and Cultural significance are not allowed.
  • OLSNepal don't allow the post ad like "Luxury items with unrealistic pricing/not corresponding to market pricing".
  • Software & movie piracy, software to unlock phones/video games or Stocks, and Other Securities are considered as an illegal activites.
  • Tickets/vouchers/passes for many events, games, matches or concerts, lottery tickets, sweepstakes entries, subscriptions, etc. are prohibited.
  • Traffic devices, which include radar detectors/hammers, license plate covers, traffic signal changers /sensors, and related products.
  • Transfer or selling license are not allowed here.
  • Used Cosmetics, undergarments, personal hygiene & grooming items (e.g. epilators, shavers, etc).
  • Weapons,guns,arms etc are not allowed to Post.
  • The government has declared face masks and hand sanitizers as essential commodities.So, OLSNepal supports this move and only allows these products at a regulated price.
  • User are don't allowed to post ad related to Covid Antigen Kits
